My First Int’l DESIGN GiG

Business Landing Page

The idea around the business landing page is to discuss both the Crowdsourcing AI platform which was designed by a different designer and the Assessment platform which was designed by me. So, the challenge here was being able to sell two different products without complicating the design with information overload. I followed the same structure as the other landing pages.However, this design took a while as concepts like the following were constantly deliberated on:

What should a user see first when they land on the page What will a user prioritize doing on the page, immediately they understand the essence of the page.

...

To solve the following, we had to prioritize the ‘skimmer’ while also considering the ‘in depth’.

The skimmer and the in depth are fictional users of the page. The skimmer just needs very little overview to take an action while the in depth, although he doesn’t fully correlate with its title, needs just a little more than the skimmer to ‘slowly’ take an action.

...

After several IAs,

having the main actionable items reside in the hero section of the page became the focal point of the business landing page.

In this context, the actionable items were the cards which talked about the individual business platforms, alongside the know more primary buttons to trigger action.

Overview of The Design Process

Conclusion

This was pretty tricky as there were several refactoring of the IA. In the end everyone agreed to the idea and that was what we handed over to the developers.
